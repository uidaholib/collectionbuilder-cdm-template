Photos from the Columbia River Basin Project featured in PBS Documentary!

Several photos from this collection were used in the production of the PBS documentary Grand Coulee Dam, which premiered as part of the American Experience series on April 3, 2012. A preview of the documentary is embedded in the viewer below.

A copy of the DVD is available at the University of Idaho Library. For additional resources and information about the movie, visit the PBS website.
