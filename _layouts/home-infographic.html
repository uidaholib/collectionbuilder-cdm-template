Description

Here We Have Idaho is the long standing official magazine of the University of Idaho. It's stories highlight the research, learning, and outreach of U of I students, employees, and alumni, showcasing their impact across the state and the world. This collection provides access to a digital archive of past issues that have been transferred to the library for preservation.

Please visit the Here We Have Idaho home page for current issues, digital subscriptions, and to learn more about this unique publication.
